## 🇲🇪 Kotor — Thursday, Jul 23 (7:30 AM–6 PM port)
### Booked: Blue Cave + Lady of Rocks tour 12–3 PM (already locked)

**Unique to Kotor (can't see elsewhere):**
- 🏔️ **Bay of Kotor (Boka Kotorska)** — Europe's southernmost fjord-like ria. Only place in the entire Adriatic with this dramatic fjord geography.
- 🧱 **Old Town city walls climb to San Giovanni Fortress** — medieval switchback walls, 4+ km, up to 260m elevation
- ⛪ **St. Tryphon Cathedral** — one of the oldest cathedrals in the Adriatic still in active use (1166), Diocletian-era relic casket

### Morning (7:30 AM – 12:00 PM) — before the booked tour

| Time | Stop |
|---|---|
| 7:30 AM | ⚓ Disembark, walk 5 min to Old Town Sea Gate |
| **7:45 AM** | ⛪ **St. Tryphon Cathedral** — Romanesque + Baroque, silver saint's casket — 30 min |
| **8:15 AM** | 🧱 **Start walls climb to San Giovanni Fortress** — 4 km switchbacks, 260m up. Start EARLY (before 10 AM). |
| **9:30 AM** | 🏔️ **Top of San Giovanni Fortress** — panoramic Bay of Kotor, ship below, mountains all around |
| 10:15 AM | 🚶 Descend (easier than climb) |
| 11:00 AM | 🥐 Café/water break in Old Town |
| **12:00 PM** | 🚤 Meet booked Blue Cave + Lady of Rocks tour |

### Afternoon (3:00 PM – 6:00 PM) — after tour

| Time | Stop |
|---|---|
| 3:00 PM | 🚤 Return to Kotor port |
| 3:15 PM | 🍽️ Late lunch at Old Town konoba — try Njeguški pršut (local prosciutto) + local goat cheese |
| **4:30 PM** | 🚶 Walk the **Old Town streets** — this is the "living UNESCO fortress" experience, cats everywhere (Kotor's famous for its cats — there's even a Cats Museum) |
| 5:00 PM | 📸 Photos on the waterfront with the ship + fjord backdrop |
| 5:30 PM | ⚓ Back on ship |

**Do NOT miss:** Walls climb + Bay of Kotor fjord view from the fortress. It's the only fjord in the Adriatic.

## 🇬🇷 Corfu — Wednesday, Jul 22 (7 AM–5 PM, rental car booked)

**Unique to Corfu (can't see elsewhere):**
- 🏝️ **Paleokastritsa emerald coves + sea caves** — accessible only by small boat, on every "top 10 Greek beaches" list
- 🏛️ **Achilleion Palace** — Kaiser Wilhelm II's Greek Revival palace, one of only two neoclassical palaces on any Greek island
- 📸 **Kanoni + Mouse Island (Pontikonisi)** — THE iconic Corfu photo, this view exists only here
- 🐟 **Corfiot Venetian-Greek cuisine** — bourdeto, sofrito, bianco — Venetian occupation left a food fusion that doesn't exist on mainland Greece

|---|---|

| 8:15 AM | 🚗 Drive to Kanoni (15 min) |
| **8:30 AM** | 📸 **Kanoni + Vlacherna Monastery + Mouse Island** — cross causeway to monastery, iconic photo — 45 min |
| 9:15 AM | 🚗 Drive to Achilleion (25 min) |
| **9:45 AM** | 🏛️ **Achilleion Palace** — Kaiser Wilhelm's palace, Achilles statue, sea-view gardens — 75 min, €10/adult |
| 11:00 AM | 🚗 Drive to Paleokastritsa (60 min through olive groves) |
| **12:00 PM** | 🍽️ **Cliffside taverna lunch** — order bourdeto (spicy fish stew) or sofrito |
| **1:30 PM** | 🏝️ **Paleokastritsa Monastery** — 13th-century clifftop, small museum, unbeatable views — 45 min |
| **2:15 PM** | 🚤 **Sea cave boat tour** — small motorboat from beach, 30 min, ~€15/person, blue caves only reachable by water |

## 🇭🇷 Split — Friday, Jul 24 (7 AM–5 PM, walking)

**Unique to Split (can't see elsewhere):**
- 🏛️ **Diocletian's Palace** — the ONLY still-inhabited Roman imperial palace in the world. Real residents inside 1,700-year-old walls. Nowhere else.
- 🎬 **Palace Basements** — Daenerys' throne room in Game of Thrones (Christian will care)
- ⛪ **Cathedral of St. Domnius bell tower** — climb inside a converted Roman mausoleum. Diocletian's actual tomb, now Christian.
- 🌊 **Picigin at Bačvice Beach** — Split's own unique ball game, invented here 1920s, played only on this specific beach in the world

### Plan

| Time | Stop |
|---|---|
| 7:15 AM | 🚶 Walk from port to Old Town (10 min) |
| **7:30 AM** | 🏛️ Enter **Diocletian's Palace** via Silver Gate |
| 7:35 AM | 📸 **Peristyle** — central courtyard, black granite sphinxes brought from Egypt by Diocletian himself |
| **7:45 AM** | ⛪ **Cathedral of St. Domnius** + climb **Bell Tower** — panoramic Split — 45 min, €7 |
| 8:45 AM | 🎬 **Palace Basements** — Roman cellars, Game of Thrones — 30 min, €5 |
| **9:15 AM** | 🏛️ **Jupiter's Temple** — now a baptistery, tiny gem hidden in the palace — 15 min |
| 9:30 AM | 🚶 Wander Cardo/Decumanus streets — Roman street grid still in daily use |
| 10:00 AM | 🚶 Exit palace via **Golden Gate** — see **Gregory of Nin statue** (rub his toe for good luck, locals do this) |
| **10:15 AM** | 🌳 **Walk up Marjan Hill** via staircase behind Riva |
| **11:15 AM** | 🏔️ **Telegrin Peak viewpoint** — 360° Split + islands + your ship in the harbor |
| 12:00 PM | 🚶 Down Marjan west trail |
| **12:45 PM** | 🍽️ **Konoba lunch** — order pasticada (Dalmatian pot roast) or fresh grilled fish — 90 min |
| **2:30 PM** | 🏖️ **Bačvice Beach** — 15 min walk from Old Town |
| **2:45 PM** | 🌊 **Watch/join picigin** — the unique local ball game, played only here. Locals play daily. Kids wade in and try. |
| 3:45 PM | 🍨 Gelato on walk back |
| 4:00 PM | 🚶 Back toward port |
| **5:00 PM** | ⚓ On ship |

**Do NOT miss:** Picigin at Bačvice. On every "unique cultural experiences in Croatia" list.

## 🇸🇮 Koper — Saturday, Jul 25 (8 AM–7 PM, rental car booked)

**Unique to Slovenia (can't see elsewhere):**
- 🏰 **Predjama Castle** — world's largest cave castle, built INTO a 400-foot cliff. Guinness World Record. Nowhere else.
- 🚂 **Postojna Cave** — Europe's largest show cave. ONLY cave in the world with a passenger train ride through it (built 1872). Home to the "human fish" (olm) — a blind salamander found nowhere else, that can live 100 years without eating.

### Plan

| Time | Stop |
|---|---|
| 8:00 AM | 🚗 Pick up SIXT rental — Koper Pristaniska 21 |
| 8:15 AM | 🚗 Drive to Predjama (55 min) |
| **9:15 AM** | 🏰 **Predjama Castle** — cliff-cave castle — 60 min, €18/adult |
| 10:30 AM | 🚗 Drive 10 min to Postojna Cave |
| **11:00 AM** | 🚂 **Postojna Cave train tour** — book online in advance — 90 min guided, €31/adult |
| 12:30 PM | 🍽️ Lunch at Postojna Cave restaurant — try štruklji, jota |
| **2:00 PM** | 🚗 Drive to Piran (35 min) |
| 2:45 PM | 📸 **Piran quick hit** — Tartini Square + Church of St. George bell tower — 60 min |
| 4:00 PM | 🚗 Drive back to Koper (25 min) |
| 4:30 PM | 🚗 Drop off rental |

**Do NOT miss:** Predjama Castle. The "wait, that's real?" moment of the trip. Photos don't do it justice.
